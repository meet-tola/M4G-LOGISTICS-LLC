Why Choose Us

We Are Professional Logistics & cargo Agency

Our logistics company specializes in Box truck, Flatbed, Dry Van, and Reefer transportation. We offer reliable and efficient transportation solutions for businesses of all sizes, whether you need to transport small packages or oversized cargo. Our services include:

  • Box truck transportation: Our box trucks are ideal for transporting small to medium-sized cargo, such as packages, furniture, and equipment.

  • Flatbed transportation: Our flatbed trucks are perfect for transporting large, heavy, or oversized cargo that cannot be transported in a standard box truck.

  • Dry van transportation: Our dry van trucks are designed to transport goods that need to be protected from the elements, such as electronics, clothing, and paper products.

  • Reefer transportation: Our reefer trucks are equipped with refrigeration units that keep perishable goods, such as food and medicine, at the right temperature during transportation

Requirements

To Ensure High-Quality Service

We have requirements for transporting goods. This includes complying with regulations, providing accurate details and documentation, and adhering to our safety protocols. We provide personalized solutions and work closely with you to ensure all requirements

Read More

  • Copy of CDL's

    Before we can transport your goods, we require a copy of the commercial driver's license (CDL) of the driver who will be operating the vehicle.

  • Certified state

    We require that all vehicles used to transport goods are certified by the state where they are registered.

  • Minimum Insurance

    To protect your goods and ensure that you are covered in case of any accidents or incidents, we require a minimum level of insurance coverage for all shipments.

  • Sign 9-W

    The 9-W form is a document that verifies that the goods being transported are not subject to hazardous materials regulations.

  • Signed Carrier Agreement

    We require a signed carrier agreement between our logistics company and the client before we can transport any goods.
